# Mini Game: Caution Me

Welcome — and thank you for stopping by. "Caution Me" is a small interactive art piece and collection of experimental sketches that invites you to gently pay attention to someone who may be quietly withdrawing. Play, explore, and reflect — this work is meant to be simple to interact with and thoughtful in feeling.

## Live demo

Open and play the piece in your browser:

https://cocokona.github.io/MiniGame-CautionMe/

## What this is about

The artwork shows a person experiencing low spirits, gradually withdrawing and closing off their inner world. The game asks you to be present — your small, caring interactions decide how the story unfolds.

## How to play

- When the caution lines appear, help by touching (clicking or tapping) them before a new caution line is generated.
- Keep paying attention to the lines as they continue to appear.
- If you stop helping, the character may fade away.

Try to keep interacting — different actions lead to different outcomes.

### Endings

- Bad ending: If you stop helping, the character may not be seen again.

- Happy ending: If you keep being attentive, the character can find some warmth and stay connected.

(Play to discover the endings yourself.)
